Innovating the Future with Advanced Nanotechnology

Our advanced Nano Liquid Solutions provide superior material performance and protection through innovative formulations, enhancing durability and resistance. We specialize in Nanotechnology for Textiles, including cotton, wool, and other fabrics, using nanocoatings to improve strength, durability, and functionality. Our Silk-Based Technology extracts high-purity sericin from silk cocoons and yarns, offering premium silk protein for industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, biomedical, and textiles. With Nanotechnology for Concrete, we improve strength and longevity while boosting resistance to environmental factors, ensuring more resilient structures. Additionally, our Aerospace Nanocoatings deliver lightweight, high-strength coatings that enhance protection, efficiency, and performance, advancing aerospace technology. Our solutions drive innovation and enhance material properties across diverse industries.​
