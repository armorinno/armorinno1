OUR PROJECTS

Silk  Nano Liquid 

​Silk nano liquid is a type of advanced nanotechnology that typically refers to a liquid solution infused with nano-sized particles derived from silk proteins. This technology is often associated with the development of products for various applications, including coatings, textiles, and even medical uses.

Leather Nano Liquid

Leather nano liquid is a protective solution that contains nanoparticles that are designed to penetrate and bond with the leather material at the microscopic level. These nanoparticles create a thin, durable protective layer on the surface of the leather, offering several benefits without altering the texture or appearance of the leather.

see more

Textile Nano Liquid

​Textile nano liquid is a specialized treatment designed to coat fabric fibers with tiny nanoparticles, which creates a protective layer on the surface of the textile. These nanoparticles are typically made of materials like silica, fluoropolymers, or other advanced compounds that improve the fabric's properties in several ways. The coating is often invisible, leaving the fabric’s appearance unchanged but providing enhanced functionality.
