​Biotechnology is the field that uses living organisms or their components to develop products and processes for specific uses, encompassing areas like medicine, agriculture, and industry. 

LEATHER

Leather is a strong, flexible and durable material obtained from the tanning, or chemical treatment, of animal skins and hides to prevent decay.

SILK

Silk, a luxurious natural protein fiber, is renowned for its softness, strength, and natural luster, making it a prized material for clothing, bedding, and other textiles. 

TEXTILE

A textile product is any material made from interlacing fibers, encompassing a wide range of applications from clothing and home furnishings to industrial and technical uses. ​
